How to Participate

Step 1.

Open the musical.ly app. Before you start recording, search and select a version of Billie Jean available in the library.

Step 2.

Record a video of your pet walking.

Step 3.

Then, click on the clock icon and choose the “reverse” option.

Step 4.

Post and share using the hashtag #AnimalMoonwalk – don’t forget to make your profile open!
